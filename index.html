Various Cat Breeds

  • Persian cat: Fluffy, calm, and loves relaxing. Needs regular grooming.
  • Siamese cat: Very talkative, social, and intelligent. Loves attention.
  • Maine Coon: One of the largest breeds. Friendly and gentle (“gentle giant”).
  • Bengal cat: Looks like a leopard! Very active and playful.
  • Ragdoll: Super affectionate and relaxed — goes limp when held.
  • Sphynx cat: No fur! Needs warmth and skin care. Very friendly.
  • British Shorthair: Quiet, easygoing, and has a teddy-bear look.
  • Scottish Fold: Known for its folded ears. Sweet and calm personality.
